What is HackSpectra?

HackSpectra is a 24-hour hackathon by M.G.M’s College of Engineering, Nanded, bringing together 45 teams of 2 to 4 members to tackle real-world challenges through innovation and collaboration. Beyond coding, participants can enjoy midnight games, a cultural night, and free goodies, making it a thrilling and unforgettable experience!

Why Join HackSpectra?

HackSpectra is more than just a hackathon—it's a thrilling experience filled with innovation, fun, and opportunities! Here's why you should join:

  • Work on real-world challenges using cutting-edge tech
  • Win amazing prizes, swags, and certificates
  • Boost your resume with hands-on experience
  • Enjoy fun activities, cultural night & midnight games

HackSpectra Rules

Follow these guidelines to ensure a fair and fun competition!

  • Teams can have 2 to 4 members.
  • Bring your own laptops and chargers.
  • No plagiarism or pre-built templates allowed.
  • 24-hour hackathon with checkpoints.
  • Internet access is provided, but no external help.
  • Projects will be judged on innovation, execution, and impact.
  • Submissions must be made before the deadline.
  • Registration fee: ₹1000 for experts, ₹800 for students.
  • Problem statements will be allocated at the inauguration ceremony.
  • Violations of rules may lead to disqualification.
